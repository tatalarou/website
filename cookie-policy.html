1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Fleex.ai ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Privacy Policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

3. Types of Cookies

3.1 Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They help websites remember what you're doing from page to page.

3.2 Persistent Cookies

Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help websites remember your preferences for future visits.

4. Cookies We Use

Fleex.ai uses only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the operation of our website and platform. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies.

5. What We Don't Use

To respect your privacy, Fleex.ai does NOT use:

  • Analytics Cookies: We do not track your behavior across our website
  • Advertising Cookies: We do not serve targeted advertisements
  • Third-Party Tracking: We do not allow third parties to track you through our website
  • Social Media Cookies: We do not use social media tracking pixels

6. Legal Basis for Using Cookies

Under GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive (EU Cookie Law), we use essential cookies based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: Essential cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our service
  • Consent: Your consent is recorded via our cookie banner for preference cookies

7. Your Cookie Choices

7.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner explaining our use of essential cookies. You can:

  • Accept: Allow essential cookies to ensure full functionality
  • Decline: You may decline, but this may affect website functionality

7.2 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

7.3 Consequences of Disabling Cookies

If you disable essential cookies, you may experience:

  • Inability to log in to your account
  • Loss of session data
  • Reduced security
  • Impaired website functionality

8. Third-Party Cookies

We do not allow third parties to set cookies through our website. All cookies are set directly by Fleex.ai and are used solely for the purposes described in this policy.

9. Data Retention
